Our Story

Born from a love of simple pleasures

Cookies & Coffee started with a kitchen table, a grandmother's recipe book, and a stubborn belief that the best moments in life happen over a warm cup and something freshly baked.

We opened our doors to give this neighbourhood a place to slow down. A place where regulars are greeted by name, where the coffee is always worth talking about, and where every cookie leaves a little happiness behind.

Everything we serve is made in-house, using locally sourced ingredients and techniques passed down through generations. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just honest, delicious food and drink.
